About EarthBound Journeys

Your trusted partner in extraordinary travel since 2012

Founded by a team of passionate globetrotters, EarthBound Journeys has spent over a decade crafting transformative travel experiences. We've walked the paths we recommend, slept in the lodges we book, and built relationships with local communities that span years.

What sets us apart? We limit our group sizes to ensure personalized attention. We prioritize sustainable tourism that benefits local economies. And we design every journey with flexibility—because the best travel moments often happen unexpectedly.
